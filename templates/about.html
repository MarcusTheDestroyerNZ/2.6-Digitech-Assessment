About the Game

The Origin

This environmental game was inspired by my work on the AS91890 assessment. During that examination, I explored concepts about environmental sustainability and realized there was an opportunity to transform those ideas into an interactive learning experience.

Why This Game?

The assessment helped me understand the importance of environmental education, and I wanted to create something that would make learning about environmental issues both fun and engaging. By turning these concepts into a game, players can learn about environmental challenges while actively participating in solutions.

From Concept to Reality

What started as theoretical work in AS91890 has evolved into this interactive game. The project combines educational content with gaming elements to create an engaging experience that promotes environmental awareness and understanding.
